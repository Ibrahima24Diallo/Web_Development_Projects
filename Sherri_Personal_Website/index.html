Sherri Williams

CEO of Sherri Williams

Sherri Williams is Vice President of Credit and Financial Services, responsible for
directing all the credit and collection activities of the company and leads a team of
credit underwriters and financial and customer service analysts. She has extensive
credit experience in wholesale distribution in the construction and petroleum
industries.

Sherri is an active member of First Baptist Church, Garner, NC. She has also held
several leadership roles in Boy Scout Troop 19 where her son earned his Eagle
rank.

She has been married to her husband Denny for over 30 years and is very proud
of their two grown children.
